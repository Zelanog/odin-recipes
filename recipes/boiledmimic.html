Boiled Mimic

Mimic Boiled with Salt

Recipe Description

It is prepared by boiling together only mimic and salt, and eaten much like other shellfish, using thin tools to dig out the chewy, flavorful meat.

Ingredients

  • Mimic upper body
  • Claws
  • Legs
  • Lots of salt

Steps

  1. Clean the mimic carcace
  2. Cut its aprts and add it on a boiler pan
  3. Add a lot of Slat and cook it
  4. Enjoy!
